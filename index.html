Welcome to my website!

I'm Bryn (known as Frigiduck in online spaces), and I make music and mouth sounds.

I've been making music since I was 14, but it became a true hobby of mine during my last year of high school. Since then, I have made music for personal projects and for my own amusement.

In addition, I've done voice work in the past for projects that friends of mine have made, the most notable one being Voices of Wynn, a mod that adds voiceover to an MMORPG Minecraft server called Wynncraft.

I have many different online accounts, including ones on YouTube, SoundCloud, itch.io, and Twitch. Links to these accounts are listed below!
